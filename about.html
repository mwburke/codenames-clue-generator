About the Author

My name is Matthew Burke, and I am using this website to share some of the things I'm interested in. During the weekdays, I work as a data scientist, and the rest of the time I like to play video games, hike, read sci-fi, watch stupid comedies, hang out with friends and family and spend far too much time shopping online for shoes but never actually buy any.

Data Science/Visualization

Taking raw data and condensing it into information either through statistical analysis or transforming it into a easily consumable visual representation is an enjoyable and ever-necessary task. Hopefully I can share cool projects or things I've learned while doing so.

Creative Coding

I recently found a whole community of people who use code to create a variety of art forms, and due to my lack of drawing capabilities/general creativity, I find myself drawn towards this. It's a great combination of structure and organized chaos, and most importantly, I don't have to embarrass myself with my lack of hand-eye coordination.

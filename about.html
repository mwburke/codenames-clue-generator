About the Author

My name is Matthew Burke, and I'm a data scientist with a passion for the intersection of machine learning, optimization and data visualization.

Education

Masters in Information and Data Science, UC Berkeley

B.S. Industrial Engineering and Operations Research, UC Berkeley

Why "Works of Chart"?

Visualization can be a powerful tool to summarize information in an easily digestible format, and so I have been forced to delve in to the world of web development in order to communicate these ideas. Fortunately, there are a lot of great resources to reduce the complexities of getting up and running, like Jekyll which is used to power this blog.

Contact

Please don't hesitate to reach out if you have any feedback on the website, ideas you'd like to see visualized, or anything else data related. The best way to do that is probably through LinkedIn.
